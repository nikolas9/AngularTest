5 advantages

that agreeing with the customer's final design gives you

1. Design helps you sell

Using the finished design, you will help the customer mentally visualize the project, see everything from inside, and have a better understanding of how it will look; enabling the customer to realize his dreams.

Do not miss the opportunity, set up a contract and start the work. This avoids the risk of something changing mid-way through the project because the customer is unhappy with how things look.

Everything had been agreed beforehand ...

2. Your defense against surprises and misunderstandings

Visualization is a document that is agreed with the customer before the start of construction. In this case you come to consensus with the customer about how the resulting construction will look.

3. You already have a plan of action and reference terms

Having agreed upon the visualization with the customer beforehand is an easy way to maintain control throughout all stages of construction.

During the working process you can show how the results should look to each specialist (electricians, plumbers, painters want).

4. All decoration materials can be purchased in advance

You will know what materials and colors will be used in construction beforehand. You will be able to plan their phased purchase in advance so the number of decisions in the process is minimized.

5. Image of how the "assembled puzzle" will look

You will immediately see the result that you want to achieve. There is no need to stop at the end of each phase and think about what to do next or agree on the details with the customer as that has already been done.

It is also a huge time saver and does not require the constant presence of the customer on the site.
